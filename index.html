Hello, my name is Granit Elshani, and I'm a highly motivated data-driven professional with a Master's Degree in Banking and Finance. Recently, I completed the Google Data Analytics course on Coursera, enhancing my skills in data analysis tools like SQL, SPSS, Python, and visualization tools.
